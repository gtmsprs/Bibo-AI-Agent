Researched digital clocks, and was inspired by the Casio BM. Sketched up a clock design and while designing, I decided how to wanted to go with a basic clock design that involves using a digital clock that counts down with 
an hour, a minute, and a second (HH:MM:SS format) I also wanted the clock to include the current date and day of the week. I also included a visual bar to represent the temperature inside my room, and I plan to make it 
battery-operated, as there are too many clocks these days that require using a plug to operate a clock.
